About us

We are the Missouri State Assciation for Machinery Computing (or MOState ACM). We want students involved with programming on campus to be able to learn from others with their same passions, we are welcome to recruiters and software developers in the community to show interesting about the industry. Everyone is welcome to come to our meetings on Thursdays at 5pm in Cheek 209 (For the semester of Fall 2015).
